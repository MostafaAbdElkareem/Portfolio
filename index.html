Who I Am

Consultant, Builder,
and UX Thinker

I'm a Microsoft Power Platform and SharePoint Consultant with over 18 years of experience turning tangled enterprise workflows into streamlined, governed digital platforms. I've worked across government agencies, financial services, energy organisations, and education — always with the same goal: build something people actually want to use.

Currently at ANSTO (Australian Nuclear Science and Technology Organisation), I'm architecting and delivering end-to-end Power Platform solutions — from complex approval systems and regulatory registers to public-facing booking portals. My background in UX/UI design means I don't just ship features; I think carefully about how every screen feels to the person clicking it.

Before Australia, I spent 14 years at ITWorx in Cairo working across Qatar, UAE, and Egypt — building SharePoint portals, leading UX teams, and working with enterprise clients including Qatar's Commercial Bank, Vodafone Egypt, and the UAE Ministry of Education.
